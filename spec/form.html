School Information

It is the practice of the WRDSB that students are considered for the the Partial French Immersion program at their home school first. If the program is not offered at your child's home school, or is full, please specify whether you would like your child to be considered for the Partial French Immersion program at another school by indicating your preferences in the drop down boxes below.

If you do not want your child to be considered for the French Immersion program at any school but your home school, leave the second and third choice options blank.

Note: The Waterloo Region District School Board does not provide transportation to students who attend a school outside their residential boundary. Thus, if your child is placed at a school outside your residential boundary (geographical home school), transportation to and from the school will be the responsibility of the parent/guardian.
